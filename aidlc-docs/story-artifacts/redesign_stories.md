# User Stories: Offline Wins - Focus Edition

## Theme: Visual Identity
**As a** user,
**I want** a "Focus" aesthetic that is clean, minimal, and tech-forward,
**So that** the app feels like a professional tool for mental clarity.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] **Color Palette**: Charcoal, Slate Blue, Cool White. High contrast for text, subtle backgrounds.
- [ ] **Typography**: `Inter` or `Geist Mono` for a precise, engineered look.
- [ ] **Motion**: Fast, fluid, non-bouncy transitions.

---

## Theme: Core Loop (Timer & Tracking)
**As a** user,
**I want** a "Breathing Ring" timer,
**So that** I can center myself before starting a session.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] Minimalist stroke ring.
- [ ] Pulses slowly (4s in, 4s out) to guide breath.

**As a** user,
**I want** a rewarding experience that isn't "gamified" (no confetti),
**So that** I feel a sense of mature accomplishment.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] **The "Ripple" Effect**: Upon completion, a gentle wave/ripple clears the screen.
- [ ] **Session Artifact**: The completed session transforms into a "Solid Block" or "Token" on the timeline, symbolizing time solidified/saved.
- [ ] **Insight**: Display a brief, data-driven summary (e.g., "You've reclaimed 2 hours this week") instead of "Good job!".

---

## Theme: Retention (History)
**As a** user,
**I want** to see my "Focus Chain",
**So that** I am motivated to keep my habit alive.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] Calendar view showing unbroken chains of focus days.
- [ ] "Total Focus Time" displayed prominently as a compounding number.
